I'm Jeongeun Ha, a Data Engineer on the AI team at Banksalad.

I like understanding the situation first, then building systems that actually get used—in the simplest, most reasonable way possible.

## Things I've built

I care a lot about building tools that non-engineers can actually use. I built an internal SERP analysis platform end to end—crawling, analysis pipelines, APIs, and web UI—cutting non-technical teams' research time from 1–2 hours to 30 minutes. I'm currently designing a pipeline that crawls and cleans data from 7 external sources in the insurance domain, connecting it as context for a RAG-based insurance consultation AI.

I also designed an MCP Server that connects Jira, GitHub, Notion, Slack, and Google Workspace so the entire organization can use AI consistently, not just individual experimenters. The starting point is always the same: understanding what's actually inconvenient for the people who need it.

## Before this

I studied Chemistry at POSTECH, then completed a Master's in AI at the same university. In between, I spent about a year at Samsung Electronics as a semiconductor photo process engineer. Chemistry to semiconductors to AI—looking back, I've always moved toward domains that are more flexible and have more to explore. I prefer being in a space where there's a lot left to learn.
