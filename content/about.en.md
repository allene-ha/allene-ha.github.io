I'm Jeongeun Ha, a Data Engineer on an AI team.

I like understanding the situation first, then building systems that actually get used—in the simplest, most reasonable way possible.

## Things I've built

I care a lot about building tools that non-engineers can actually use. I built an internal data analysis platform end to end—crawling, pipelines, APIs, and web UI—significantly cutting non-technical teams' research time. I'm currently designing a pipeline that crawls and cleans data from multiple external sources, connecting it as context for a RAG-based AI service.

I also designed an MCP Server that connects internal tools so the entire organization can use AI consistently, not just individual experimenters. The starting point is always the same: understanding what's actually inconvenient for the people who need it.

## Before this

I studied Chemistry, then completed a Master's in AI. In between, I spent about a year as a semiconductor process engineer. Chemistry to semiconductors to AI—looking back, I've always moved toward domains that are more flexible and have more to explore. I prefer being in a space where there's a lot left to learn.
